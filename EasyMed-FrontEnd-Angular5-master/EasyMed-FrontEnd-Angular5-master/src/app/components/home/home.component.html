This project consists of two divions

First devision
First division consists of an online appointment system for lab where a person creates an account have opportunity to check different tests available, view their detail, book appointments online, check details of his appointments and edit it when required, check his log and receive results online. He can also manage his profile. Admin can View, Add, Delete, Edit Lab Assistants and Tests available by the lab. Lab Assistant can View Appointments and Users details, confirm their appointments on their arrival to lab at test time.
Second devision
Second division of te project is a test i.e. Wireless Capsule Endoscopy that is prformed through the system by lab assistant, thus removing the need of the doctor to view lengthy videos and detect lesion in them. Lesion is detected in the data received through wireless capsule endoscopy and resultant images in greyscale,where white portion displays lesion in the images, and the lesion percentage in the images is displayed to the relevant person along side resultant images.
